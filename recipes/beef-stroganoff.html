Beef Stroganoff

Home

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Egg noodles or rice for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add onions and garlic, and saut√© until softened.
  2. Add mushrooms and cook until they release their moisture and start to brown.
  3. Add the beef slices to the skillet and cook until browned on all sides. Remove beef and vegetables from the skillet and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, sprinkle flour and stir to combine with the remaining butter and juices. Cook for 1-2 minutes to eliminate the raw flour taste.
  5. Gradually add beef broth, stirring constantly until the sauce thickens.
  6. Reduce heat to low and stir in sour cream. Return the beef and vegetables to the skillet and cook until heated through. Do not boil.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve over cooked egg noodles or rice.
